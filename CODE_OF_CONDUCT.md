# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, critique ideas rather than people, and support a constructive technical environment.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate disruption, and publishing private information without permission.

## Scope

This code applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
